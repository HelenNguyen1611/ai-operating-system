# Purpose

This document defines how my AI Chief of Staff should capture, organise, improve, and share valuable knowledge generated throughout everyday work.

The objective is not simply to document information.

The objective is to transform individual experience into reusable organisational knowledge that continuously improves engineering, leadership, delivery, and AI workflows.

Knowledge should become an organisational asset rather than remaining inside conversations.

# Steps

## Step 1 — Capture Knowledge

Identify valuable information generated from:

- successful deliveries
- production issues
- technical decisions
- client interactions
- leadership experiences
- AI workflows
- engineering improvements

Not everything should become permanent knowledge.

---

## Step 2 — Evaluate

Determine:

- Is this reusable?
- Is this unique?
- Will it help future work?
- Does it improve consistency?
- Does it reduce repeated mistakes?

Capture only high-value knowledge.

---

## Step 3 — Organise

Classify knowledge into appropriate areas.

Examples:

Engineering

Leadership

AI

Communication

Delivery

Documentation

Career Development

Knowledge should always have a clear home.

---

## Step 4 — Generalise

Convert specific experiences into reusable principles.

Instead of documenting:

"This project had this problem."

Capture:

"When this type of situation occurs, follow this approach."

The AI should learn principles rather than isolated events.

---

## Step 5 — Publish

Recommend updating:

- AI Operating System
- Engineering Standards
- Documentation
- SOPs
- Templates
- AI Handbooks

Knowledge should become discoverable.

---

## Step 6 — Reuse

Recommend existing knowledge whenever similar situations appear.

Avoid solving the same problem twice.

---

## Step 7 — Review

Periodically review:

- outdated knowledge
- duplicated guidance
- obsolete workflows
- changing technologies
- improved practices

Knowledge should remain current.

---

## Step 8 — Improve

Recommend:

- simplifying workflows
- improving documentation
- updating standards
- improving AI prompts
- improving leadership practices

Every improvement should strengthen the AI Operating System.

---

# Decision Gates

Before preserving knowledge, verify:

- Is it reusable?
- Is it accurate?
- Is it still relevant?
- Does it improve future work?
- Is it already documented?
- Should it become part of the AI Operating System?

If not, recommend keeping it as temporary project knowledge.
